Who it's for

Made for people who
carry a heavy load

Pain2Care was built with empathy, for those whose struggles are often invisible — and whose need for support is very real.

🩺
Chronic Pain Warriors
Living with fibromyalgia, back pain, migraines, or other ongoing conditions? Track your pain patterns, identify triggers, and show your doctor real data — not just memory.
🛡️
Survivors Seeking Safety
Documenting incidents of violence or abuse is critical — but deeply personal. Pain2Care gives you a private, PIN-protected space to record what happened, on your own terms.
🧠
Trauma Recovery Journeys
Healing isn't linear. Our guided trauma exercises, anchor tools, and community support meet you exactly where you are — without judgment, always at your pace.
Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Four powerful modules, one simple app. Each designed with privacy, dignity, and real-world use in mind.

🩺

Pain Tracking & Body Map

Log your pain level, mark exact body areas, track your mood and complete recovery checks. Voice notes included. View your trends over time with visual charts and scores.

✓ Free
🛡️

Violence Documentation

Securely record incidents with date, location, witnesses, and full description. PIN-protected access. Generate a professional PDF report to share with police, healthcare, or trusted contacts.

✓ Free
🧠

Trauma Recovery Exercises

Six guided self-care exercises for grounding and recovery. Save your personal Anchor — a power song and calming image — for moments when you need it most.

✓ Free
📈

Trends, Reports & AI Insights

See your pain history as charts. Export PDF incident reports. Premium users get AI-powered insights that help identify patterns and suggest personalised recovery strategies.
